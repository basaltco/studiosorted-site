Why use StudioSorted?

Most software guides for martial arts schools are written by the software companies. Every review here is based on original research — vendor pricing pages, verified Capterra and G2 reviews, and the specific things that bite a UK club rather than an American one. Including the catches vendors would rather you found out later.

Martial arts first, not gym software

Half the tools marketed at dojos are gym platforms with a belt field bolted on. We judge everything on gradings, rank and syllabus — not generic class scheduling.

The UK-specific problems

Direct Debit and GoCardless, GBP versus USD pricing, and association licence admin. The things a US-written review will never mention.

Catches are mandatory

Every review has a documented catches section and a "who this is not for". Sourced from real user reviews — never invented.

Discipline differences respected

A BJJ academy tracking four stripes per belt and a karate dojo running a kyu ladder are different businesses. We don't flatten them.
